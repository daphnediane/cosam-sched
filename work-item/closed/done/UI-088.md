# UI-088: Sort guest presenters first in presenter filter dropdown

## Summary

Guest presenters should appear at the top of the presenter filter dropdown,
above panelists and groups.

## Status

Completed

## Priority

Medium

## Description

The presenter dropdown currently shows all individual presenters in a single
alphabetical block. Guests (those with `rank: "guest"`) are mixed in with
panelists alphabetically, making them harder to find for users who primarily
want to follow their favorite guests.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The presenter dropdown has a "Guest Presenters" section at the top (or
  equivalent grouping) listing only presenters with `rank: "guest"`.
- Remaining individual presenters follow in their own section.
- Groups remain at the bottom as before.
